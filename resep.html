Carrot Cake Recipe

Carrot Cake

This is a soft, moist, and flavorful carrot cake!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and grease a baking pan.
  2. Mix sugar, oil, and eggs until smooth.
  3. Add grated carrots and vanilla extract.
  4. In another bowl, mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.
  5. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  6. Pour batter into the pan.
  7. Bake for 30–35 minutes.
  8. Let it cool, then serve.
